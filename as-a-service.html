Analysis-as-a-Service

Our Analysis-as-a-Service subscription is a recurring monthly program of analysis service provided to your organisation.

Our Analysis-as-a-Service subscriptions are best suited to small-medium organisations who want the flexibility of having top analyst and project management professionals on-hand, without the ongoing commitment of permanent staff.

The greatest benefit of our subscription model is the flexibility - feel free to scale-up and down every month as required in order to suit your business, your pipeline of works, and your immediate needs.

By maintaining an ongoing subscription with Backbeat, you'll benefit from not only having access to our analyst and project management professionals, but also our Futurists and Innovation Specialists and their ongoing subscriber-only content.

Analysis-as-a-Service FAQs

How will the service be provided?

Our analysts and project management professionals work directly with you from within our Remotely Integrated Office, which you are invited to join us in during your allocated Analyst hours.
Backbeat maintains and manages a backlog of work for each client to ensure that the Analyst or Project Manager is focussed on the right priorities.

Can we change subscription tiers?

You have full control over your monthly subscription, and can scale up or down as required through our subscription portal.
At the start of each monthly billing period, you will be billed up-front for the current subscription that you're on.

Where are Backbeat's analysts and project managers located?

All our analysts and project managers are hired within Australia, and may either work within our Remote Integrated Office, or on-site at your location depending on your subscription tier, location, and accessibility.
We are an equal opportunity employer, and as a result, should your workplace be unable to provide the accessibility accommodations necessary for your allocated highly qualified analyst or project management professional, then the services will be provided via our remote integrated office.

How will the analysts know what to work on?

At the start of every month, a backlog prioritisation session will be held, regardless of your subscription tier. At this session, your backlog will be prioritised and effort will be allocated. The number of hours allocated to your individual tickets will depend on your chosen subscription tier. Our analysts estimate time against each prioritised ticket, which will identify how many hours effort will be required to complete the task.

Can we cancel our subscription?

Yes, your Analysis-as-a-Service subscription can be cancelled at any time via our subscription management portal.

How do we sign up for an Analysis-as-a-Service subscription?

We suggest contacting us first to make sure that our Analysis-as-a-Service program is the right type of engagement for your particular needs. We are available for a fee-free initial consultation to discuss your needs and what we can offer to support you and your business.
