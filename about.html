Our Services

At backbeat, we take pride in being part of your success, because that is how we operate. Analysis is our bread and butter, and that is where we specialise - from generic business analysis right through to specialist disciplines across a wide variety of distinct areas. By engaging with backbeat, you'll have access to some of the best talent in the country, and that's a win for you.

The world of analysis is broad, and we specialise in the areas that are critical for today's success and tomorrow's reward.

Our specialist areas are focussed on building ongoing business success

  1. Business Analysis - Our Business Analysts assess the current approach and apply expert analysis & ideation skills to design new and improved solutions across business process, capability and technology.
  2. Project Management - Our Project Managers are experts in planning, organising, and directing all aspects of your projects and initiatives from idea right through to delivery.
  3. Strategy Analysis - Our Strategic Analysts are experts in helping businesses identify needs and opportunities, and developing actionable plans that target the direction of success.
  4. Innovation Analysis - Our Innovation Analysts are experts in innovative technology, market trends & analysis, competitor analysis and investment analysis, and work hard to help you innovate and build an innovation pipeline.
  5. Trust Analysis & Trust Management - A new discipline in the analysis field, our Trust Analysts focus on deep-diving into the core trust components across the value chain in order to find opportunities to improve commercial outcomes through trust management.
  6. Journey Mapping & Journey Auditing - Design and audit customer and staff journeys across all aspects of your business to ensure your organisation doesn't fall victim to unconscious discrimination. Our D&I Analysts optimise, audit and design customer & staff experience journeys that consider accessibility, diversity, quality, efficiency and innovation.
  7. Futures Thinking & Foresight Analysis - Our Futurists are on hand to design future scenarios specific to your organisation in order to intentionally challenge the status quo and help you innovate and define your future growth strategy.
