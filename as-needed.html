Analysts as Needed

Time for something new? Got a new idea? Recent acquisition? New business direction? New competitors in the market?
Whatever the need, make sure you kick-off your next phase with the right talent.

Backbeat analysts and project management professionals can be secured to work on your next 'big thing' without any ongoing commitment.

As part of our agreement, you'll be allocated a team of talent that can be deployed amongst your internal teams to assist in delivering your project or initiative. Each engagement is based on a fixed scope or duration, and there's no ongoing commitment beyond the terms of the contract.

Collaborative

Our allocated teams will work alongside you in whatever capacity you require, but with that we bring our expertise in analysis and in running successful projects. We don't just walk in, deliver, and leave - we are about building sustainable success through all our engagements.

Committed

We always provide the highest value of work ethic, performance and expertise, and that is our commitment.
Throughout the duration of the fixed-scope or fixed-duration engagement, our teams are committed to helping you and your teams succeed.

Consistent

All Backbeat talent follows our Insight - Foresight - Resight framework, which is guaranteed to ensure that, regardless of the specific project or initiative, the delivery will be of quality, it will be lasting, and it will be sustainable into the future.

Analysts as Needed FAQs

How will the service be provided?

Our analysts and project management professionals work directly with you from within our Remotely Integrated Office or on-site with your in your office location.
Our 'as needed' agreements operate as a fixed scope/duration piece of work, whereby our team is embedded within your organisation to assist in the delivery of a defined project or initiative.
All of our client partners are assigned a dedicated Partnership Manager, who will provide you with ongoing support, drive value, and ensure partnership success throughout the term of the engagement.

Is there any ongoing commitment?

Our 'as needed' engagements are fixed scope and/or fixed term. There is no requirement to continue engaging with Backbeat after the conclusion of a specific engagement; however, many clients change to an 'Analysis-as-a-Service' or 'on Retainer' model to have an ongoing engagement with Backbeat.

What kinds of projects/initiatives can Backbeat be engaged for?

Backbeat analysis and project managers can be engaged for any type of business-related projects - it could be the implementation of a new technical solution, organisational restructure, operational improvement, strategic transformation, growth initiatives, and strategic planning.
