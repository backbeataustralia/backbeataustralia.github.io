Engage us in the way that suits you

We offer a variety of different engagement models to suit how you do business. We are flexible and supportive, and able to meet you where you're at, and help you take the next step.

To find out which option best suits you, click to find out more about each of our engagement options.

Analysis-as-a-Service

Choose one of our tiers of Analysis-as-a-Service programs to access our Analysts in an ongoing agreement. Our Analysis-as-a-Service program gives you guaranteed access to our team of expert Analysts. Our 'as-as-service' subscriptions are best suited for small-to-medium businesses.

Analysts on Retainer

An upfront monthly retainer to secure ongoing access to our expert Analysts for an agreed number of hours every month. Our retainer agreements are best suited to organisations that have a larger pipeline of work that require ongoing access to our skilled analysts.

Analysts 'as Needed'

Our 'as needed' arrangements are more suitable when distinct projects or specific needs arise and access to our expert Analysts is needed for a fixed duration. 'As Needed' arrangements have no ongoing commitment beyond the agreed scope; however, organisations can easily transition over to our 'analyst-as-a-service' or 'analysts on retainer' agreements upon completion.
