Your squad becomes an integral part of your business - working alongside you and your teams to help in your success.

Analysts on Retainer FAQs

How will the service be provided?

Our analysts and project management professionals work directly with you from within our Remotely Integrated Office or on-site with your in your office location.
Our 'on retainer' agreements operate like a partnership, with backbeat effectively becoming a division of your business - we help you strategise, operate and deliver.
All of our 'on retainer' partners are assigned a dedicated Partnership Manager, who will provide you with ongoing support, drive value, and ensure partnership success.

How many hours can we access on retainer?

Each 'on retainer' partnership is scoped uniquely with each client so as to ensure that the right level of access is available and to ensure the partnership delivers value.
We design our retainer agreements off your pipeline of work and baseline the forecast average of hours per month that will be required to support your organisation. As our retainer programs allow for a degree of rollover flexibility, we are able to assist in providing a constant monthly rate should one month be more demanding of our services than the next, therefore keeping costs predictable and easy to budget.

Where are Backbeat's analysts and project managers located?

All our analysts and project managers are hired within Australia, and may either work within our Remote Integrated Office or on-site at your location.
We are an equal opportunity employer, and as a result, should your workplace be unable to provide the accessibility accommodations necessary for your allocated highly qualified analyst or project management professional, then your allocated squad will be balanced between on-site and remote members.

How will the analysts know what to work on?

At the start of every month, a backlog prioritisation session will be held with you and your backbeat squad leader. At this session, your backlog will be prioritised and effort will be allocated. The number of hours allocated to your individual tickets will depend on your chosen subscription tier. Our analysts estimate time against each prioritised ticket, which will identify how many hours effort will be required to complete the task. Based on the quoted effort for each ticket, the backlog will be prioritised into fortnightly sprints for ongoing delivery.

Can we cancel our retainer?

As our 'on retainer' partnership are based on an ongoing contract, the exit terms will be dependent on each individual agreement.

How do we sign up for an 'Analysts on Retainer' partnership?

Entering into an 'Analysts on Retainer' partnership is a longer-term commitment, and as a result, we would like to meet personally with your team to ensure we're the right fit for your organisation and our 'on retainer' partnership is the best engagement option for your particular needs.
We are available for a fee-free initial consultation to discuss your needs and what we can offer to support you and your business.
