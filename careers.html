Build a successful career with Backbeat

Working with Backbeat guarantees you ongoing career progression and professional development within your chosen analysis or project, production or operations management field.

We guarantee what others don't - a career where you can learn, grow, and succeed. Because we want to grow and retain the best analysts and management professionals in Australia.

What can you expect from a career with Backbeat?

  • $10k towards your professional development allocated on your anniversary date every year - that's enough to get you to your professional industry conference in Florida, plus more, every year!
  • Transparent salary based on years of experience and loyalty - annual salary progression guaranteed
  • Internal mentoring from distinguished career professionals to support you in your chosen analysis or management profession

  • We provide an inclusive and accessible working environment and encourage all talent to consider a career with Backbeat - we want to build a diverse client-base, so we want the best talent across the country, and to secure it, we'll provide all the accommodations necessary to help you succeed
